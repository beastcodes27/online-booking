About SafariExpress

SafariExpress is a modern bus ticket booking platform offering convenient, safe, and reliable travel options across Tanzania. Our aim is to digitize public transport booking and make travel easier for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital ticketing platform in East Africa by ensuring a smooth and reliable booking experience for every traveler.

Our Mission

To connect people and places through innovation, safety, and excellent customer service in the transport industry.
